12 /**
13 * The class @c Number is the superclass of platform
14 * classes representing numerics values that are convertible
15 * to the primitive types @c gbyte, @c gshort, @c gint, @c glong,
16 * @c gfloat, @c gdouble.
17 *
18 * For platform classes, the conversion is often analogous to a
19 * narrowing primitive conversion or a widening primitive conversion
20 * for converting between primitive types. Therefore, conversions may
21 * lose information about the overall magnitude of a numeric value, may
22 * lose precision, and may even return a result of a different sign
23 * than the input.
24 */
25 class Number : public virtual Object
